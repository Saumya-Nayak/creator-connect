Introduction

Our commitment to your privacy

At Creator Connect, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and services.

Our Promise: We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our data practices. Your trust is important to us.

Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website, mobile applications, and any related services (collectively, the "Platform"). By using Creator Connect, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Last Updated: January 18, 2026. We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Data We Collect

Information you provide and we gather

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services to you:

Information You Provide

Account Information

Name, email, username, password, profile picture

Content Data

Posts, images, videos, comments, and creative work

Payment Information

Billing details for transactions (processed securely)

Communications

Messages, support requests, feedback

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (with your consent)
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: To remember preferences and analyze usage

How We Use Your Data

Purposes of data processing

We use the information we collect for various purposes to provide, maintain, and improve our services:

  • Provide Services: Create and manage your account, process transactions, deliver content
  • Personalization: Customize your experience, recommend content and connections
  • Improve Platform: Analyze usage patterns, develop new features, fix bugs
  • Communication: Send updates, newsletters, promotional materials (with consent)
  • Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, protect user safety
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations, enforce terms, resolve disputes

Legal Basis: We process your data based on consent, contractual necessity, legal obligations, and legitimate interests in accordance with applicable laws.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

When we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

With Your Consent

We share information when you explicitly give us permission, such as when you connect third-party services or share content publicly.

Service Providers

  • Hosting & Infrastructure: Cloud storage and computing services
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment processing services
  • Analytics Providers: To understand platform usage and improve services
  • Communication Services: Email delivery and messaging platforms

Public Content: Information you post publicly (portfolio, posts, comments) can be viewed by anyone and may be indexed by search engines.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, safety, or the safety of others.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

How we use cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our platform and store certain information.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function (authentication, security)
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use the platform
  • Marketing Cookies: Track marketing campaigns and deliver relevant ads

Cookie Control: You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

Other Tracking Technologies

We may also use web beacons, pixel tags, and similar technologies to track user activity and improve our services.

Your Privacy Rights

Control over your data

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (right to be forgotten)
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Object: Object to certain types of data processing

Exercise Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@creatorconnect.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Note: Some rights may be subject to limitations based on legal requirements or business needs.

Children's Privacy

Protection for younger users

Creator Connect is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our platform.

Age Requirement: Our platform is intended for users aged 13 and older. Users under 18 must have parental or guardian consent to use our services.

COPPA Compliance

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

  • Parental Controls: Parents can request access to their child's information
  • Deletion Rights: Parents can request deletion of their child's data
  • Marketing Restrictions: We don't send marketing to users under 18

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@creatorconnect.com so we can delete it.

International Data Transfers

Cross-border data handling

Creator Connect operates globally. Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Your data is transferred using approved mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

  • Legal Safeguards: We use appropriate safeguards for international transfers
  • Data Subject Rights: Full GDPR rights including portability and erasure
  • Legal Basis: Processing based on consent, contract, or legitimate interest

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of personal information.

Do Not Sell: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
